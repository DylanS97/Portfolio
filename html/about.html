Who Am I?

My name is Dylan Stevenson and I am from Manchester, England. I have always had a passion for electronics and computers and I spent my teen years always taking apart phones, consoles, controllers and any other electronics that would happen to break so I could see how they work.

I then started to come across computer programming videos and started to find a passion there. I started to learn programming in my spear time during 2019 where I started learning C# and produced a few simple projects in my spare time. I then came across a free course from Harvard by the name of Introduction to Computer Science where I developed skills in C, Python and basic skills in HTML and CSS.

At the beginning of 2021, I came across Netmatters that offer the SCS Scheme and it is here that I developed a much better understanding of HTML, CSS and JavaScript and also gained new skills in PHP and Laravel.

My Goals

My current goals are to achieve a career in back-end development and develop my skills further in PHP and Laravel.

I also want to venture into machine learning and game development when I get more competent with the languages and frameworks I am currently learning.
